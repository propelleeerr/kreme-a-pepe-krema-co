About Us

Welcome to Kreme-a-Pepe, where we celebrate Filipino flavors in a fun and unique way! Our specialty, the Kreme-a-Pepe, is a coin-shaped Taiyaki inspired by the Philippine peso, filled with delicious local ingredients. We are a passionate team of student entrepreneurs dedicated to sharing the taste of home while connecting with our community. With every bite, we aim to bring a smile and a sense of belonging, making our delightful treats a perfect choice for anyone looking to enjoy a flavorful twist on tradition.

Our Mission

  1. Celebrate Filipino Culture: To honor and preserve our rich culinary heritage by offering innovative, delicious, and culturally significant food products that resonate with the hearts of our customers.

  2. Promote Community Engagement: To actively engage with our community by participating in local events, supporting initiatives, and providing unique food experiences that bring people together.

  3. Deliver Quality and Affordability: To provide high-quality, flavorful snacks that are accessible and affordable for everyone, ensuring that our products can be enjoyed by students, families, and food enthusiasts alike.

  4. Encourage Culinary Exploration: To inspire individuals to embrace the fusion of traditional and modern flavors, making Kreme-a-Pepe a must-try for those looking to embark on a delicious culinary adventure.
