Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is the product called "Kreme-a-Pepe"?

The name “Kreme-a-Pepe” playfully combines the words “Kreme” with the nickname of Jose Rizal, "Pepe,” which symbolizes the commitment of the company in celebrating and promoting Filipino heritage through its unique offerings.

2. What inspired the creation of Kreme-a-Pepe?

As student entrepreneurs, we became inspired to create Kreme-a-Pepe from our passion for Filipino culture and a desire to share it with others, globally. Growing up surrounded by the rich flavors of our heritage, we recognized the need for a product that not only highlights traditional Filipino tastes but also innovates them to cater to all audiences.

3. What types of products does Kreme-a-Pepe offer?

At Kreme-a-Pepe, we offer a signature product that showcases a variety of beloved traditional Filipino flavors. Our delicious treat features the following flavors:

• Ube - A vibrant purple yam flavor that is a staple in Filipino desserts, bringing a sweet and earthy taste to our offering.

• Condensed Milk - A creamy, rich flavor that adds a delightful sweetness, reminiscent of classic Filipino desserts.

• Leche Flan - A smooth and rich caramel custard flavor that captures the essence of this beloved Filipino delicacy.

Each of these flavors is crafted and then grilled in a unique mold designed to resemble the Philippine piso coin.

4. Are there vegetarian or vegan options available?

Yes! At Kreme-a-Pepe, we are committed to inclusivity and are excited to offer vegetarian and vegan options. We believe everyone should enjoy the beloved flavors of our heritage, and we are actively working to create delicious plant-based treats.

5. Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes! At Kreme-a-Pepe, we understand the importance of dietary needs and are pleased to offer gluten-free options. Enjoy worry-free indulgence with Kreme-a-Pepe!

6. Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we partner with local delivery services to provide convenient options for our customers. You can find us on various delivery platforms such as Foodpanda, GrabFood, and Hungrily.

7. Where can customers find Kreme-a-Pepe products?

Customers can find Kreme-a-Pepe products both on-campus and online. We sell our treats at Stanislaus Chin’s Cafeteria at Saint Joseph School, building connections with our community. Additionally, our website offers easy online ordering and payment, with convenient delivery options.

8. Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events and celebrations. Our products not only deliver delightful flavors but also feature a visually appealing presentation that adds a unique touch to any gathering.

The best way to stay updated on Kreme-a-Pepe's news and promotions is to follow us on social media! You can also subscribe to our newsletter on our website for exclusive updates, special offers, and announcements.

10. How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We greatly value your feedback and encourage you to share your experience with us. You may provide your insights through the contact form available on our website or by messaging us via our official social media channels.
