Kreme-a-Pepe
Kreme-a-Pepe takes the shape of the Philippines' piso coin, featuring the face of Jose Rizal, highlighting its goal of promoting Filipino culture. The delicious, coin-shaped treats can be purchased individually, making them an ideal choice for a quick snack on the go. For those who prefer to share or are looking for a simple gift or pasalubong, we also offer bundles, which include 3 pieces.
